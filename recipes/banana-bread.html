Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Walnuts
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, butter, eggs, flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl, and stir until smooth.
  2. Add bananas and walnuts, and transfer to a baking pan.
  3. Bake it in the oven and cool loaf before serving.
